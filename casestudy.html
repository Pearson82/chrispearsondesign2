Victoria and Albert Museum

Ticketing kiosk interface

A simple, intuative interface for purchasing exhibition tickets in the V&A’s new entrance

Earlier this year, with only two months until the new V&A Exhibition Road Quarter opened it’s doors to the public, I was given the rather urgent task of designing a digital interface, to allow visitors to buy tickets from self-service kiosks.

The concept had been conceived some time ago, by A_LA, the architects of the new Exhibition Road Quarter. As part of that ambitious vision, 8 large 56″ display screens would be joined side by side, to form a continuous horizontal display.

Process

Over several months, we defined the system architecture, competitor landscape, user experience, brand identity and visual language. The result? A tool that simplifies the processing of big sets of data, so users can ask questions, discover new insights and extract real value.
