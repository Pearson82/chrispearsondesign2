About me

I am a London based digital designer. Currently design lead at the Victoria and Albert museum, throughout my career I’ve designed for large organisations like the Guardian, the British Museum and the FT, as well as small design agencies and direct with clients.

Trained in graphic design and typography, I’ve spent many years immersed in digital technology, developing a passion for human centred design and crafting user interfaces.

More about me

Outside of work, I love to travel and be outdoors. I’m usually dreaming up an adventure, or training for a challenge of some kind. I’ve run multiple marathons, cycled many miles across many countries. I also enjoy nothing more than food, beer and the company of my wife and friends.
